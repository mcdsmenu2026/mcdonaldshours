What Are McDonald's Hours in Canada? Opening and Closing Times Explained

Have you ever driven to a McDonald's only to find it closed? It is really frustrating, and it happens more than you think. The reason is simple — McDonald's hours in Canada are not the same at every location. Most McDonald's restaurants are owned by regular people, not by the McDonald's company itself.

These owners can choose their own opening and closing times. So one McDonald's in Toronto might open at a different time than one in a small town in New Brunswick, even on the same day. This guide will help you avoid that problem.

You will learn the usual open and close times, when breakfast ends, whether the drive-thru stays open later than the inside, and what happens on holidays. We will also show you the quickest way to check the hours of the exact McDonald's you want to visit. No more guessing and no more wasted trips.

What Are the Standard McDonald's Hours in Canada?

Most McDonald's restaurants in Canada open somewhere between 5:00 AM and 11:00 PM. Many drive-thrus stay open all day and all night, so you can get breakfast, lunch, or a late-night snack any time. But the exact hours can be different depending on what province or city you're in.

Restaurants in busy downtown areas or next to highways often stay open later. Restaurants in small towns may close earlier. Here's something many people don't know: these are just usual patterns, not promises.

An owner running a McDonald's in a quiet town might open at 6:00 AM and close at 10:00 PM, simply because that's all the business they get there. So if you plan to go very early in the morning or late at night, it's smart to check the hours first instead of guessing that your local McDonald's follows the usual schedule.

What Time Does McDonald's Serve Breakfast in Canada?

Breakfast starts around 5:00 AM at most McDonald's and ends at 10:30 AM on weekdays. On weekends, you get a little more breakfast time, since breakfast lasts until 11:00 AM. That extra half hour on Saturday and Sunday matters more than you'd think.

It can mean the difference between getting a hot Egg McMuffin with your coffee or showing up just as they switch to lunch food. As of 2026, McDonald's Canada does not serve breakfast all day long. Once a restaurant switches to lunch, breakfast is over.

McDonald's did try serving breakfast all day, starting back in 2015, but they stopped doing that everywhere in Canada in 2020. Since then, they haven't said they plan to bring it back. You can still get some McCafé drinks and baked snacks at any time of day. But if you want real breakfast food, you need to get there before the cutoff time. Even showing up ten minutes late means you'll have to wait until the next morning to try again.

Are Any McDonald's Locations Open 24 Hours in Canada?

Yes, but not as many places stay open all night as people think. Some McDonald's, especially in big cities like Toronto, Vancouver, and Montreal, stay open 24 hours a day. But this is usually only through the drive-thru.

These spots are normally near highways, hospitals, bus or train stations, or busy parts of the city, where enough people show up late at night to make it worth staying open. Here's the important part: a 24-hour drive-thru does not mean you can walk inside and eat at any hour.

At many of these restaurants, the inside seating area closes well before midnight, even though the drive-thru stays open all night. So if you're planning a late-night food run, plan on using the drive-thru only. To check if the inside is open too, use the McDonald's Canada app or the restaurant locator.

This mixes people up a lot, again and again. Here's the key thing to remember: the drive-thru almost always stays open longer than the inside seating area. So if the doors to walk in are locked, the drive-thru might still be open. Hours can also change on weekends or holidays. Some restaurants open later than normal, and some stay open longer than normal too.

Weekend hours at McDonald's are usually the same as weekdays. Some locations open a little later on Sunday mornings. The biggest changes happen on holidays. McDonald's is usually open on days like New Year's Day, Canada Day, and Thanksgiving, but they often close earlier than normal. Since every location has a different owner, hours can vary from place to place.

Just type "McDonald's near me" into Google Maps. It will show you the hours, the busiest times, and whether the place is open right now. FAQs - McDonald's Hours

  1. What time does McDonald's open in Canada?
    Most McDonald's open between 5:00 AM and 6:00 AM. If the drive-thru is open 24 hours, it never closes. McDonald's inside malls usually open later, around 9:00 AM or 10:00 AM, because they follow the mall's schedule.
  2. What time does McDonald's stop serving breakfast in Canada?
    Breakfast usually ends at 10:30 AM on weekdays and 11:00 AM on weekends. After that, they switch to the lunch menu and breakfast is no longer available.
  3. Is McDonald's open on Christmas Day in Canada?
    Most locations stay open on Christmas Day, but with shorter hours, usually around 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Hours can vary by location, so check the app before you go.
  4. Is McDonald's open on Boxing Day in Canada?
    Most locations stay close to their normal hours on Boxing Day. The drive-thru tends to be busier than usual. Check your local McDonald's on the app to confirm the hours.
  5. Does McDonald's have different hours on Sundays in Canada?
    Sunday hours are usually similar to Saturday hours. Some locations open a little later, around 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM. Breakfast still ends at 11:00 AM on Sundays.
  6. How do I find the closest 24-hour McDonald's in Canada?
    Open the McDonald's Canada app, use the restaurant locator, and filter by "Open 24 Hours" to find the nearest one.
  7. Are McDonald's drive-thru hours longer than dine-in hours?
    Yes, most of the time. The drive-thru usually stays open later than the inside seating area. At 24-hour locations, the drive-thru never closes.
  8. Can I order through Uber Eats if my local McDonald's dine-in is closed?
    Sometimes yes. Many locations still offer delivery through apps like Uber Eats, DoorDash, or the McDonald's app even when the inside is closed. Check the delivery app to see if your store is available.

Conclusion

McDonald's Hours in Canada follow a normal pattern, but no two restaurants are exactly the same. Most McDonald's open between 5:00 AM and 6:00 AM. They serve breakfast until 10:30 AM on weekdays.

They close somewhere between 10:00 PM and midnight. Drive-thrus usually stay open longer than the inside seating. On holidays, hours often get shorter. And since each restaurant has its own owner, that owner is the one who really decides the hours, not the big McDonald's company.

The best thing you can do is spend 30 seconds checking the McDonald's Canada app before you leave home. It's free, it shows the real hours for your restaurant, and it stops you from guessing wrong. Google Maps works okay as a backup, but for holidays and last-minute changes, the official app or a quick phone call will always give you the best answer.
